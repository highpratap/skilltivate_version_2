About Skilltivate

At Skilltivate, we believe that a well learned society, can enable its people to lead a fulfilling life. People in their mid careers today, are not equipped to catch up to AI, which is under a continuous exponential curve of new research and development. Accountants, Programmers, Lawyers, Analysts, even farmers and Movie Actors are under a threat of being replaced by latest developments in technology and AI.
There is a desperate need to re-skill these professionals on a continuous basis to help them find new opportunities and verticals to build/rebuild their careers. Skilltivate is a platform to help people develop such skills. Be it dance, music, creative writing, pottery, sports, contemporary research and tech education, or any other form of a creative pursuit, from any part of the world, we endeavor to make it possible for you to gain access to learning the same.
